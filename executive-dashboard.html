FAQ & Help
🚀 Getting Started
Once the Urban Paws admin team assigns a booking to you, it will appear in your My Bookings page automatically. You'll see the customer's pet details, address, service type, date, and time slot. Make sure your profile is up to date so admins can assign the right jobs to you.
Go to My Bookings, find the booking, and use the status dropdown to change it from Confirmed to Completed. Only mark it completed after you've finished the service. The customer and admin will be notified automatically.
Wait at least 10 minutes and try calling the customer using the phone number shown in the booking. If there's no response, contact the Urban Paws support team immediately. Do not leave without confirmation. Mark the booking status as Pending until resolved.
💰 Earnings & Payments
Earnings are calculated based on completed bookings. Payouts are processed by the Urban Paws admin team, typically on a weekly or bi-weekly basis. You can track your total and monthly earnings in the My Earnings section. For any payout queries, reach out to our support team.
Your earnings are based on the booking amount for each completed job. The amount shown in My Earnings is the full booking value. The Urban Paws admin team may apply a platform fee depending on your agreement. Contact support for your specific commission structure.
Cancelled bookings are generally not eligible for payment. However, if the cancellation occurs after you've arrived at the customer's location, please contact the Urban Paws team — late cancellations may qualify for a partial fee at the admin's discretion.
⭐ Performance & Rating
Your rating is based on customer feedback after each completed session. Customers rate their experience out of 5 stars. High ratings lead to more assignments and better job opportunities. Always be punctual, professional, and caring with the pets to maintain a high rating.
Late arrivals, poor pet care, incomplete tasks, rude behaviour, or failure to follow the customer's instructions can lower your rating. Urban Paws monitors executive ratings regularly — consistently low ratings may result in fewer assignments or removal from the platform.
📅 Schedule & Availability
Yes! Update your availability in My Profile → Availability (e.g., "Mon–Sat, 7am–7pm"). The admin team uses your availability to assign bookings that fit your schedule. Keep this updated, especially if you take time off.
Contact the Urban Paws admin team as early as possible — ideally at least 24 hours before the booking. Do not simply ignore or miss a confirmed booking. Repeated no-shows can result in deactivation from the platform.
Mention your preferred service area in your Bio or Availability field in My Profile. The admin team will do their best to assign bookings near your location. If you're assigned a booking too far away, contact support to discuss reassignment.
🪪 Documents & Profile
Urban Paws is committed to the safety of pets and customers. Identity verification is mandatory for all executives. Your documents are stored securely and are only visible to the Urban Paws admin team — they are never shared publicly or with customers.
Executives with incomplete profiles may receive fewer or no booking assignments until the profile is fully filled in. Go to My Profile and fill in your personal info, identity documents, education details, and upload your resume to improve your chances of getting assignments.
